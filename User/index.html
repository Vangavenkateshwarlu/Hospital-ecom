Health Precautions to Stay Healthy

  • Wash your hands regularly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Wear a mask to prevent the spread of germs.
  • Maintain social distancing of at least 1 meter from others.
  • Eat a balanced diet with fruits and vegetables.
  • Exercise regularly to stay fit and active.
  • Get regular check-ups to monitor your health.
